## The Problem: Blind Spots in Competitive Pricing

E-commerce brands live and die by pricing. But most are flying blind. They check competitor sites one by one, copy numbers into spreadsheets, and hope they catch changes before their margins take a hit.

It doesn't scale. It's boring. And it's always out of date by the time anyone acts on it.

## The Solution: VantageDash

VantageDash is a competitive intelligence platform built from the ground up for e-commerce teams who need to know what competitors are charging, right now.

### How It Works

**Drop in a competitor URL. That's it.**

VantageDash auto-detects the online store platform and pulls the full product catalog automatically. No API keys, no special access, no setup headaches. Works across Shopify, WooCommerce, Uline, Ecwid, Wix, Squarespace, and more.

**AI matches your products to theirs.**

Different brands call the same product different things. VantageDash's matching engine handles naming variations, bulk vs. single pricing, and cross-platform differences. Every match comes with a confidence score so you know what to trust.

**Get alerts the moment prices change.**

Slack ping. Email notification. Your call. Set custom thresholds so you only hear about the changes that matter.

### What You Get

- **Price trend charts** showing how competitor pricing moves over time
- **Competitor heatmaps** for a bird's-eye view of the market
- **CSV and PDF export reports** for team sharing and presentations
- **Role-based team access** with enterprise audit logging
